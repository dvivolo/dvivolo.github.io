About Me

I’m a DFIR-focused cybersecurity analyst with 4–5 years of combined experience across public sector environments. My background includes SOC operations, incident triage, threat detection, and compliance frameworks such as NIST 800-171 and CMMC.

One of my core strengths is building clarity where there isn’t any — I’ve written dozens of detection response guides, alert triage workflows, and draft playbooks to help security teams move from reactive to proactive. I’m process-driven, deeply analytical, and enjoy translating complex security signals into actionable documentation that enables faster and more confident analyst response.

I’ve also supported compliance readiness, internal audits, and vulnerability remediation projects, especially in environments with fragmented tools and limited automation.

Currently, I’m actively expanding into DevSecOps — learning Terraform, IaC security patterns, and secure CI/CD pipelines — with a focus on Microsoft Azure and SecurityX certification prep. I bring a blend of detection logic, documentation discipline, and infrastructure awareness to every project I touch.

Outside of work, I’m a detail-obsessed problem solver who enjoys tinkering with systems, organizing chaos, and occasionally escaping into fantasy fiction or game worlds where I don't have to write documentation for everything (yet).
